The records of 6400+ graves can be searched, sorted, browsed, and downloaded (as a comma-separated value file) below.

A partial map of the cemetery from 1982 is available, courtesy of W&L Special Collections and Archives.

Disclaimer: The data here is incomplete and has not been kept up date with new internments or gravestone cleaning projects. It is recommended that you verify information in Find a Grave or through other sources.
